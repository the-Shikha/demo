# DocConnect - Transforming Healthcare Access

DocConnect is an innovative health consultation platform designed to bridge the healthcare gap in underserved communities. Leveraging AI, telemedicine, and mental wellness tools, DocConnect makes quality healthcare accessible, affordable, and just a tap away.

## 🌟 Features

### 🏥 Seamless Doctor Consultations
- **Online Video Consultation**: Users can book virtual appointments with verified doctors.
- **Offline Clinic Visits**: Schedule appointments with doctors at nearby hospitals and clinics.
- **Real-time Notifications**: Users receive booking confirmations via email and in-app notifications.

### 🤖 AI-Powered Health Assistance
- **Symptom Checker**: AI chatbot, powered by Gemini API, provides instant medical insights.
- **Health Queries**: Get AI-driven responses for common health concerns.

### 💊 Medicine & Prescription Management
- **Upload Prescriptions**: Easily purchase medicines via integrated pharmacies.
- **Discounted Health Products**: Access affordable healthcare essentials.

### 🧠 Mental Wellness Support
- **Therapy Sessions**: Connect with psychiatrists for mental health support.
- **CBT-Based AI Therapy**: AI-driven support for stress and anxiety management.
- **Wellness Games** *(Upcoming)*: Engage in laughter-activated games and a Virtual Pet Companion to enhance emotional well-being.

### 📍 Location-Based Services
- **Find Nearby Healthcare Centers**: Locate hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies using Google Maps integration.

### 📅 Easy Appointment Management
- **"My Appointments" Section**: Track upcoming consultations and access past medical records.
- **Notifications & Reminders**: Get updates on scheduled appointments and important health alerts via Firebase Cloud Messaging.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Frontend & Backend**: Flutter
- **Authentication & Database**: Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Database)
- **AI & NLP**: Gemini API
- **Video Consultation**: Google Meet (Doctors send meeting links to patients)
- **AI & Backend Processing**: Python, TensorFlow
- **Navigation & Location Services**: Google Maps API

## 🚀 Future Plans
- **Electronic Health Records (EHR)**: Secure digital health records for better medical history tracking.
- **AI-Driven Disease Detection**: Early diagnosis using AI-based predictive analysis.
- **Gamified Mental Wellness Tools**: Interactive wellness games to promote emotional well-being.
- **Expanded Doctor & Pharmacy Network**: Onboarding more healthcare professionals and service providers.
